## USACO notes

- monthly, starting december
- 1 weekend block to participate
- 4 hours for normal contest, 5 hours for US open(final contest)
- 4 levels, you advance by doing good on pervious level, start on bronze
- python is fine for lower levels, c++ is ideal
- python may be too slow to solve all problems outside bronze level
- previous problems are available [here](https://usaco.org/index.php?page=training) - they support trying them and will grade submissions
- gold & platinum contests should be started within 15 minutes of when the problem becomes available (saturday 9am)
